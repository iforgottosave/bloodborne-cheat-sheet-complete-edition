1st Floor Sickroom

  • Kill or run past the Scourge Beast
  • In the next room to the left are Blood Vial x2
  • Open the door to the courtyard and grab Quicksilver Bullets x10
  • Open the gate to Central Yharnam

Hunter's Dream

  • If you died to the Scourge Beast at the start, you will wake up here. Otherwise access this area through the Central Yharnam Lamp in a bit
  • Choose your Weapon (Saw Cleaver, Hunter Axe, or Threaded Cane) from the Messengers on the stairs
  • Choose your Firearm (Hunter Pistol or Hunter Blunderbuss) from the Messengers on the stairs
  • Collect the Notebook from the Messengers on the stairs
  • Talk to Gehrman
  • Interact with the first Headstone of Awakening to the right of the stairs and awaken either at the 1st Floor Sickroom Lamp or the Central Yharnam Lamp

Central Yharnam

  • Head down the short stairs to the terrace and grab Blood Vial x4 to your left. Admire the Great Bridge ahead
  • Turn back, and now your path will take you to some old carriages. The gate on your right is locked so go down the path to the left to the end of the road to grab Molotov Cocktail x6
  • Activate the nearby lever to drop down a ladder. Don't forget to grab near the lever Blood Vial x2
  • Light the Central Yharnam Lamp
  • Talk to Gilbert, Critically Ill Man to begin his questline. His window is the one with a red lantern directly in front of the Lamp
  • From Gilbert's window, head right and down the stairs to grab Pebble x8
  • Up ahead take a left through the broken fence onto the upper level of the street and grab Molotov Cocktail x4
  • Head down the stairs onto the main street then head left until you reach the closed gate from before. Pull the lever to the left of the gate to open it, then head back up the street and take a right to the carriage in front of the steps you descended earlier. Head up the stairs and then take the stairs to your right and grab Blood Stone Shard x1
  • Head back down the stairs to the upper street level and head to the right. Continue until you can grab Quicksilver Bullets x5
  • Drop down through the broken fence at the end of the upper street. There is a locked gate at the end of the main street where you can grab Blood Vial x2
  • Take the stairs on your left while facing the locked gate up to the upper street and at the start of the path is Coldblood Dew (1)
  • Head through the archway and immediately to your left will be some barrels hiding Blood Stone Shard x1
  • Directly in front of you past the tree you can grab Blood Vial x2
  • NOTE: To your left where there are many crates piled up past the stairs there is a broken fence you can go through. We get to the area it leads to later so DON'T roll through it yet but do remember its location
  • Down the stairs and to your left are coffins you can destroy to grab Oil Urn x2
  • Turn around and face where the locked gate was. Here you can grab Molotov Cocktail x4 and the Torch
  • Turn around and head to the fountain. To your right will be stairs that you can go up. Turn right again, and head forward to a gate and grab Coldblood Dew (1)
  • Turn back and walk to the stairs you just went up on, then take a right up some more steps to an area with a well and a bench with Blood Vial x6
  • Go up the stairs to be led up to the Great Bridge and to your left hidden behind some statues will be Blood Stone Shard x1
  • From where you took the stairs up, head forward and turn left to go down some stairs. To your right on a ledge overlooking the burning effigy from the main street will be Pebble x10
  • Turn around, and there will be some crates you can roll through to grab Blood Stone Shard x1
  • To your left while facing the burning effigy will be some crates and bags shielding Blood Stone Shard x1
  • Go back to the broken fence that you were told NOT to roll through. Roll through it and go down the stairs and take a left to a pathway that leads to Coldblood Dew (3)
  • Cross the small bridge, and there will be a door with a red lantern and a Dog barking at said door. Interact with the door to speak with the Lonely Old Woman. Exhaust her dialogue, and answer 'No' for now. An alternative option will present itself later
  • Go back across the small bridge, and up the stairs until there is a fork in the path. Take the left path going up and pick up Coldblood Dew (1)
  • Go back down and take the right path going forward under an archway. Walk through the archway and open a door to a house. To your left in the dark will be Pungent Blood Cocktail x2
  • Turn around and to your left will be a door that leads outside and up the first set of stairs and to your left will be Blood Stone Shard x1
  • Up the next set of stairs will be a gate that can be opened to the Lamp. Talk to Gilbert if you haven't already
  • Head back down the stairs through the gate you just opened and head back into the house. Go upstairs and turn right to grab Coldblood Dew (1)

Great Bridge (Optional)

  • Go out the upstairs door to the left of the stairs you just ascended to end up on the Great Bridge. Heading to your left is an arch that leads to the Cleric Beast boss fight. If you have the Old Hunter Bell which you can get in the Hunter's Dream from the Messengers once you've gained 1 Insight, you may summon Old Hunter Father Gascoigne by the fountain in the Fountain Plaza past the locked gate on the main street. Additionally, you may also summon Old Hunter Alfred near the stairs through the gate at the Central Yharnam Lamp near Gilbert's window
  • Defeat the Cleric Beast to acquire the Sword Hunter Badge
  • Congratulate yourself on beating the first boss of Bloodborne and light the Great Bridge Lamp

Central Yharnam (Continued)

  • Go back across the Great Bridge and down the stairs to your right, going past the stairs leading into the house. Take a left and destroy the coffins on your left to access a ladder. Drop down onto the next level of rafters. Look out towards the Sewers and drop down one more level, being careful not to fall off. To your right will be a ladder. Drop down onto the stony walkway where the ladder meets it. Walk to the end of this pathway and there will be a beam you can drop onto to pick up Thick Coldblood (5)
  • Walk to the end of this part of the Sewers, and at the ledge to your left will be the Hunter Set
  • Drop down into the Sewer and then into the sewage to grab Quicksilver Bullets x10
  • Take the ladder in front of you up onto a wooden platform. Then take the tall ladder up to a small area with coffins and a bridge. Take a right to grab Madman's Knowledge x1
  • Turn around and go across the small bridge. There will be a ladder to your left. Go up the ladder, and at the top is a lever you can pull to open the gate that leads to the Fountain Plaza from before. Here is a window with a red lantern near the gate. Speak to the Young Girl and agree to find her mother. She will give you the Tiny Music Box
  • Go through the gate you just opened and make your way to the area with the Lonely Old Woman and go into the Dry Docks. To your right will be several barrels you can destroy to access a window. Go through this window to drop onto some rafters. Stick to the right until you find an open window. Go through it and you will find Eileen the Crow near the terrace. Talk to her and she will give you Bold Hunter's Mark x4. Talk to her again and she will grant you the 'Shake off Cape' gesture
  • Go back through the open window to the rafters area. Here there are two corpses you can cut down. Grab Coldblood Dew (1) from here as well
  • Here you must jump down onto the main area below. Near the Rifle Huntsman against the wall you can grab Coldblood Dew (1)
  • Go through the doorway on the other end that leads out into the Sewers area. At the start of the walkway you can grab Coldblood Dew (1)
  • At the end of the walkway that opens to the large Sewer area with the tall ladder will be some coffins hiding Oil Urn x2
  • Go back through the doorway and drop down onto the stone walkway parallel to the canal. Facing in from the doorway and depending which side you drop down onto you will be able to grab either the Saw Spear or Blood Stone Shard x2 from the corpses you cut down earlier
  • Drop down into the Sewers and at the end you will find Madman's Knowledge x1
  • Head out through the canal back into the main Sewer area outside. Take a right and go up the first ladder. Take a right around the walkway to grab Coldblood Dew (1)
  • Walk around to the last small bridge overlooking the large Sewer area, and here you will be able to drop down onto a rafter with Blood Stone Shard x1
  • Drop down into the Sewer Canal and turn around to go up the ladder leading back into the Dry Docks. Drop down onto the stone walkway opposite to the one you previously dropped down onto. Pick up either the Saw Spear or Blood Stone Shard x2 from the corpses you cut down earlier
  • Go out into the Canal and drop down into the large open Sewer area. Go down the stairs to your right and in the first room to your right to grab Blood Stone Shard x1
  • At the end of the outdoor Sewers will be a ladder to the right of an archway. Take the ladder up to find a large bridge. To your left will be a shortcut lift you can take up to Central Yharnam
  • Take the lift back down. Make your way across the bridge with the burning boulder. Past that to your left up a couple flights of stairs is a ladder you can climb down to an entrance that will let you sneak up behind the Maneater Boar and backstab it for an easy kill. Collect here the Saw Hunter Badge
  • Grab from here as well Coldblood Dew (2)
  • Going forward, there will be a doorway to your left that leads to a ladder. At the top to your left will be some stairs you can go up on to grab Bold Hunter's Mark x2

Tomb of Oedon

  • Go up the stairs that lead to a large graveyard. Defeat Father Gascoigne to acquire the Oedon Tomb Key
  • Light the Tomb of Oedon Lamp
  • On a rooftop near the gate is the Red Jeweled Brooch. You may crush it for a Red Blood Gem or give it to the Young Girl to immediately end her questline
  • Unlock the gate using the Oedon Tomb Key and go up the stairs and to the right then through a tunnel into a water-filled room and up the ladder into a small Library. There will be a chest here with the Blood Gem Workshop Tool
  • Unlock the gate using the Oedon Tomb Key and go up the stairs and to the right then through a tunnel into a water-filled room and up the ladder into a small Library. There will be a chest here with Kin Coldblood (12)

Oedon Chapel

  • As you enter Oedon Chapel, there will be the friendly Oedon Chapel Dweller to your right. Talk to him to allow the sending of survivors to the Chapel. Additionally, you may talk to the now-impostor Iosefka in Iosefka's Clinic and she will also offer you the option to send survivors to her
  • Go back to the Lonely Old Woman in Central Yharnam. Talk to her and tell her to go to Oedon Chapel or to Iosefka's Clinic. If you send her to Oedon Chapel, talk to the Oedon Chapel Dweller to receive the 'Triumph' gesture. If you send her to Iosefka's Clinic, talk to Iosefka to receive Numbing Mist x2
  • Talk to the Young Girl. If you did not give her the Red Jeweled Brooch, which ends her questline, choose to send her either to Oedon Chapel or to Iosefka's Clinic. If sent to the Clinic, Iosefka will reward you with Lead Elixir x2. If sent to Oedon Chapel, reload the area and kill the Maneater Boar in the tunnel in the Central Yharnam Sewers and it will drop the Red Messenger Ribbon
  • Talk to Gilbert in Central Yharnam to receive the Flamesprayer
  • Back in Oedon Chapel head outside the doorway directly in front of the Lamp and go around to the right to grab Madman's Knowledge x1
  • Go up the stairs to grab the Wooden Shield
  • Go back into Oedon Chapel and go out the side entrance. Down the stairs and behind you to the right you can grab the Top Hat Hunter Set
  • Here you can also grab Blood Vial x6
  • Go up the stairs to your right by the well. There will be a gate and lever to your left. Pull the lever to lift the gate
  • By this lever will be Blood Stone Shard x2
  • If you like, you may return to the Hunter's Dream and buy the Hunter Chief Emblem for 10,000 Blood Echoes from the Bath Messengers. Requires Cleric Beast defeated. NOTE: The Cathedral Courtyard Gate does not require the Emblem if you open it from inside, which we will do shortly after the Healing Church Workshop
  • Go down the stairs through the gate to an area with a carriage and to the right behind the carriage is a Monocular
  • Continue down the pathway to grab Blood Stone Shard x3
  • Further along the path will be a Church Giant and a Wandering Nightmare that drops Blood Stone Shard x3
  • In this area you can grab Blood Stone Shard x5
  • Go down some stairs to the right of the pathway to a room with large urns. Just beyond the room is a chest with a Tempering Blood Gemstone (1)
  • Go down the large stairs to a courtyard and head to the right to grab Coldblood Dew (2). Grab Molotov Cocktail x4 from here as well
  • Walk into the building with a tomb and at the back will be Madman's Knowledge x1
  • Go outside and facing the building head to the left and up the stairs. At the top to your right will be a door to open, and inside take another right to an overlook with a lever. Pull the lever. From here to the left will be Madman's Knowledge x1
  • Go back to where you took a right to get to the lever and head forward outside. Take a right outside and walk all the way forward to find Alfred, Hunter of Vilebloods. Talk to him and choose to 'Cooperate'. Receive Fire Paper x3 and the 'Pray' gesture
  • Go back to the lever overlook and drop down. Go down the stairs that were opened by the lever. At the bottom will be a Tempering Blood Gemstone (1)
  • Go down the stairs to a dark area then down the wooden stairs to a ladder. Descend the ladder and go up the stairs behind it. Here will be Antidote x4
  • Go down the stairs and take a right into an alcove with some large urns. Destory these to find Pungent Blood Cocktail x3
  • Light the Old Yharnam Lamp
  • From here we will head into Old Yharnam. If you wish you can come to explore Old Yharnam later. Here we will proceed as it will be a bit easier now rather than later, and the same for some other optional areas we will visit before fighting Vicar Amelia

Here will be a Kidnapper that appears once you've defeated Blood-starved Beast. Allow it to kill you, and you will be transported to the Hypogean Gaol. NOTE: Being killed by any Kidnapper in the game (i.e. the Kidnapper in the Healing Church Workshop) will also take you to Hypogean Gaol
  • Open the door to the cell you're in and turn right and go into the broken bars to grab Thick Coldblood (6)

Talk to her while wearing Healing Church garb (such as Gascoigne's Set or the Black Church Set) and she will give you Madman's Knowledge x1. You may send her to Oedon Chapel and she will grant you the 'Church Bow (Female)' gesture once you talk to her there. If you send her to Iosefka's Clinic, Iosefka will reward you with Numbing Mist x2. If you do not send her to either location, after defeating the Shadow of Yharnam her body can be found here with the Oedon Writhe Rune (Tier 1)
  • Go back up the stairs and turn left and continue up the spiral stairs. On the left set of stairs will be a Wandering Nightmare that drops Twin Blood Stone Shards x3
  • Go up the stairs all the way and light the Hypogean Gaol Lamp. NOTE: If you were killed by a Kidnapper earlier in the guide, you may now warp back to where you were and continue from there, or you may finish Hypogean Gaol now; neither choice will affect overall progression
  • In the center of the hall you can grab the Moon Rune (Tier 1)
  • Go outside and take a left up some stairs then another left and to your right will be Frenzied Coldblood (8)
  • Turn around and go back down the stairs and take a right down a corridor. Here will be a Wandering Nightmare you can kill for Twin Blood Stone Shards x3
  • Go up the stairs and to the left to open the door to the Lamp. Go back down the corridor and take a left on the road then take a left to an alcove under a small bridge to grab Bolt Paper x5
  • Continue down the road and at the end you can grab the Tonitrus
  • Turn around and walk all the way back to the large staircase and descend it. At the bottom by the gate you can grab a total of three Madman's Knowledge x1
  • To your left will be a small staircase. Climb it and to your left will be Frenzied Coldblood (8)
  • On the carriage here you can grab the Yahar'gul Black Set
  • Go back to the doorway that leads into the hall with the Lamp and take the left facing the door. Continue until you can descend some stairs. Head down and to the left and grab Twin Blood Stone Shards x2
  • Turn around and grab Frenzied Coldblood (7)
  • Open the door that leads to a prison-type room. To your left will be Blood Vial x6
  • In the center of this room against the wall will be Quicksilver Bullets x10
  • Go into the room with two tables via the left or right entrances. Go through the broken bars and take a right through a tunnel to drop onto a stony pathway leading to an open area. To your right you can summon Defector Antal. You may also summon Old Hunter Yamamura the Wanderer if you have killed him in the Hunter's Nightmare and have joined the League, but neither Antal nor Yamamura can be summoned simultaneously
  • Defeat Darkbeast Paarl to acquire the Spark Hunter Badge
  • Light the Graveyard of the Darkbeast Lamp
  • Open the gate leading to Old Yharnam

Cathedral Ward

  • Go through the Old Yharnam Gate and head up the stairs to your left, making your way to Djura without him detecting you. If done successfully, he will be friendly. Speak with him and choose to 'Spare the beasts of Old Yharnam', and he will give you the Powder Keg Hunter Badge and the 'Brush off Dust' gesture and remain friendly. If you wish, you may now kill him to unlock the Insight Bath Messengers selling the Ashen Hunter Set. Note that if you attack any of the enemies in Old Yharnam after earning his trust, he will turn aggressive with no chance of reconciliation
  • Approach Djura in secret up the tower. If you make peace with him, he will give you Bold Hunter's Mark x1 if you already have his Badge
  • Warp to the Cathedral Ward Lamp and go up the stairs where we opened the gate to Oedon Chapel. Climb the ladder near this gate and at the top grab Numbing Mist x6 at the top
  • Here you can drop down onto a roof, then another roof to grab the Black Messenger Hat
  • Drop down onto the stairs. Go up into the area with the ladder, then up some narrow stairs to grab Thick Coldblood (5)
  • Go back down the narrow stairs and take a right. Open the gate via lever to access the Grand Cathedral Courtyard. Turn around to find behind you Thick Coldblood (5)
  • To the right of the gate facing the Grand Cathedral Courtyard and behind is an area with a Dog. Grab Thick Coldblood (5) from here
  • Enter the Grand Cathedral Courtyard. From here you can grab from a total of five Madman's Knowledge x1
  • Head forward to open the gate that would have required the Hunter Chief Emblem
  • Turn around and to your left will be Thick Coldblood (4)
  • To your right from where you passed the gate will be Thick Coldblood (5)
  • Go through the doorway with the candles to the left of the second gate facing in. To your right will be some coffins hiding Twin Blood Stone Shards x2
  • To your left going in is a small room with Shining Coins x12
  • Continue through the room to end up outside on a small circular area. Here on an altar will be a Tempering Blood Gemstone (2)
  • To your right will be Antidote x1

Gestures

  • Approval: Speak with Eileen the Crow after defeating Henryk
  • Church Bow (Female): Speak with Adella after she moves to Oedon Chapel to get the gesture
  • Shake off Cape: Talk to Eileen the Crow for the first time in Central Yharnam or outside of Oedon Chapel in Cathedral Ward
  • Pray: Speak with Alfred for the first time and agree to cooperate with him
  • Wait: During the Blood Moon, speak with Eileen the Crow at the top of the Grand Cathedral stairs in Cathedral Ward before entering to face the Bloody Crow of Cainhurst
  • Beg for Life: After reaching Lecture Building, 2nd Floor, go down the ladder to Patches' lab and speak with Patches the Spider
  • Curtsey: Speak with Arianna in the foggy alley of Cathedral Ward and tell her about Oedon Chapel. Speak with her again in the Chapel to receive the gesture
  • Church Bow (Male): Obtain the Unopened Summons from the Queen's Chamber in Forsaken Cainhurst Castle and give it to Alfred in the Cathedral Ward
  • Make Contact: Step out onto the balcony in the Upper Cathedral Ward and inspect the charred body there
  • Respect: Kneel before Annalise, Queen of Castle Cainhurst in Forsaken Cainhurst Castle and join the Vilebloods
  • Triumph: Speak with the Oedon Chapel Dweller in the Cathedral Ward after having sent someone there
  • Roar: Speak with Alfred in the Queen's Chamber in Forsaken Cainhurst Castle
  • Shhh!: Speak with Eileen the Crow outside of Oedon Chapel in the Cathedral Ward and listen to her warning about Henryk
  • Brush off Dust: Encounter or defeat Darkbeast Paarl in Hypogean Gaol and enter Old Yharnam from the back, then climb up Djura's Tower without him detecting you and talk to him. Choose 'Spare the beasts' to get the gesture

  • League Oath: Crush one or more Vermin then speak with Valtr in the Forbidden Woods

FAQ

The foundation of this guide was based on Zachary Kjellberg's Dark Souls 3 Cheat Sheet code for use with Gameplay Vault's Walkthrough Guide and SlothsLoveDoom's Progression Guide

Is this a guide?

Not really. This is a set of checklists and information that you can use while playing Bloodborne to make sure you don't miss an item, action, conversation, or boss. If you need a walkthrough, you can click on the zone titles to read the wiki walkthrough

Does this playthrough need to be followed in this exact order?

Bloodborne is not a linear game and has multiple ways of progressing. This guide highlights zones as they become available, so feel free to follow however you please. You can always check things off in a different order

Can this be used for multiple characters?

Yes. Use the profile selector and buttons in the Options tab at the top of the page to setup multiple profiles
